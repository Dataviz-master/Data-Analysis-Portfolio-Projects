## Facebook Ads Performance

### CPC and CPA by Age Group

| Age Group | CPC ($) | CPA ($) |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| 25-34     | 7.46    | 16.29   |
| 35-44     | 6.91    | 15.48   |
| 45-54     | 5.97    | 12.95   |
| 55-64     | 6.65    | 14.16   |

**Key Insights:**
- Lowest CPC: 45-54 age group ($5.97)
- Lowest CPA: 45-54 age group ($12.95)
- The 45-54 demographic shows the most cost-effective performance

### Performance by Creative Type

Two creative formats were tested:
- **Carousel Ads**
- **Image Ads**

Both formats show measurable CPC and CPA metrics, allowing for optimization based on creative performance.

## Key Findings

### Google Ads
- Generic keywords consumed the majority of budget (52%)
- Brand protection remained a priority (35% of spend)
- Competitor targeting represented targeted investment (13%)
- CPC varies significantly by age group and device type

### Facebook Ads
- Most efficient demographic: 45-54 age group
- CPA ranges from $12.95 to $16.29 across age groups
- Both Carousel and Image creatives show viable performance
- Younger demographics (25-34) show higher acquisition costs

---

## Recommendations

Based on the campaign data:

1. **Budget Allocation:** Consider the strong performance in the 45-54 age group on Facebook
2. **Device Optimization:** Analyze device-specific performance on Google to optimize bids
3. **Creative Testing:** Continue testing Carousel vs Image formats to identify top performers
4. **Keyword Strategy:** Evaluate the 52% generic spend efficiency vs conversion rates
5. **Seasonal Trends:** Account for CPC fluctuations observed throughout the campaign period
